About Us

Eco Nature Zone started as a neighbourhood group and has grown into a volunteer-powered organisation restoring urban and peri-urban natural areas. We work with local councils, schools and property owners.

We believe small actions create big change. Join us to learn practical restoration skills and meet like-minded nature-lovers.
